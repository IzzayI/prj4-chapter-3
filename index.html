History

The poodle is a beautiful dog breed, very smart, intelligent and it comes in big and small. The name of the poodle comes from the German word pudeln, which means: splash in water. The poodle used to be a popular breed for water-hunts of noblemen before the 19th century, therefore the name. Because of his great attributes, the Pudel contributed to many other breeds like the Schnauzer, or the Bichon.
