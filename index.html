The Poodle

The poodle is a beautiful dog breed, very smart, intelligent and it comes in big and small.

History

The name of the poodle comes from the German word pudeln, which means splash in water. The poodle used to be a popular breed for water-hunts of noblemen before the 19th century, therefore the name. The exact country of origin is officially France, though.
